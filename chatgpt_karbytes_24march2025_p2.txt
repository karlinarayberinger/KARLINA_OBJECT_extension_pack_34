D‚ÇÉ or D‚ÇÑ (‚Äúwhich-path‚Äù detectors): If an idler clicks at D‚ÇÉ, it could only have come from slit B; if it clicks at D‚ÇÑ, it could only have come from slit A. Thus D‚ÇÉ and D‚ÇÑ provide which-path information about the signal photon.
